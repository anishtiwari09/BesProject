BES celebrated Engineer’s Day /Vishwakarma Day on 17th September, 2013

 

Broadcast Engineering Society (India) celebrated Engineer’s Day /Vishwakarma Day on Tuesday the 17th September, 2013 at 4.30 PM at Constitution Club, Rafi Marg, New Delhi.

Mr. Jawhar Sircar, Chief Executive Officer, Prasar Bharati was kind enough to be the Chief Guest. The dias was adorned by Shri Jawhar Sircar, Chief Executive Officer, Prasar Bharati, Shri Shri B.B. Pandit, Member Finance, Brig. (Retd.) V.A.M. Hussain, Member, Personnel, Shri V.K. Singla, Chairman, Seminar Committee, Shri R.K. Budhraja, Engineer-in-Chief, AIR,  Shri A. Robert Jerard Ravi, Advisor, TRAI, Mr. Ravinder Singh, Director,  Juniper Net  and Shri P.K. Singh, Hon. Secretary BES (I)

The programme started with lighting of lamp and adoration of Lord Vishwakarma by dignitaries. The speakers deliberated on various technical and policy issues in the field of broadcasting.

Shri jawhar Sircar, Chief Executive Officer honored Padma Shri H.S. Jolly, former Engineer-in-Chief, AIR and President BES (I) and Shri Shiv S. Sharma, former, DG: Doordarshan by presenting a memento and shawl on the occasion.  

Mr. P.K. Singh, Hon. Secretary proposed the vote of thanks.
